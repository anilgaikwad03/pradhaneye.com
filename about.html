Pradhan Eye Hospital & Lasik Laser Center Founded in 1984 by Dr. Uma Pradhan

Pradhan Eye Hospital began as a modest three-room clinic and has grown into a modern, spacious, NABH-accredited Eye Hospital offering comprehensive eye care under one roof. Dr. Uma Pradhan performed Solapur’s first intraocular lens (IOL) implant, marking a significant milestone in the city’s ophthalmic history. Our commitment to excellence has driven us to continually adopt the latest technologies and expand our facilities, ensuring state-of-the-art treatments at affordable costs. We offer a full spectrum of diagnostic and therapeutic services, including advanced laser technologies for accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and thorough follow-up of various eye conditions.

Our dedicated eye bank brings hope to patients suffering from corneal blindness, and in 2013, we enhanced our refractive surgery offerings by introducing the cutting-edge Amaris 200e Excimer Laser—one of the best technologies available in vision correction.

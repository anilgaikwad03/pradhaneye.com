Pradhan Eye Hospital & LASIK Laser Center was founded in 1985 by Dr. Uma Pradhan.

What began as a modest three-room clinic has transformed into a modern, spacious, NABH-accredited eye hospital offering comprehensive eye care services. Dr. Pradhan performed Solapur’s first intraocular lens (IOL) implant, a significant milestone in the city’s ophthalmic history.

Our continuous commitment to excellence drives us to adopt the latest technologies and expand our facilities. We provide a full spectrum of diagnostic and therapeutic services with advanced laser technologies, ensuring accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and complete follow-up for various eye conditions.

Our dedicated eye bank brings hope to patients with corneal blindness. In 2013, we introduced the cutting-edge Amaris 200e Excimer Laser, elevating our refractive surgery offerings with one of the best vision correction technologies available.

Mission

Pradhan Eye Hospital is committed to providing comprehensive eye care services with key emphasis on personalized treatment. Our goal is to enhance vision, health, and lifestyle through expert clinical care and surgical excellence.

Vision

To become the leading eye care center in the region, recognized for clinical excellence, innovation, and patient satisfaction by consistently upgrading our knowledge and technology.

Contact Lenses

We offer a wide range of contact lenses to suit different vision needs and lifestyles:

  • Hard Lenses: Traditional rigid lenses that provide clear vision and are durable.
  • Gas-Permeable Lenses: Allow oxygen to reach the eye; suitable for high prescriptions or astigmatism.
  • Daily-Wear Soft Lenses: Comfortable lenses worn during the day and removed at night.
  • Extended-Wear Lenses: Designed for continuous use, even during sleep (as advised by your eye doctor).
  • Colored Lenses: Add a subtle or bold color change to your eyes, with or without vision correction.
  • Disposable Lenses: Single-use lenses that offer maximum convenience and hygiene.
  • Cosmetic Lenses: Used to change or enhance eye color, even without vision correction.

Our team will help you choose the best type based on your prescription, lifestyle, and preferences.
